Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the internship?
Typically 3 to 6 months, depending on the project and academic schedule. We offer both summer internships and semester-long programs to accommodate different academic calendars.
Is the internship paid?
Unfortunately NO, we don't have any compensation or stipend money.
Can international students apply?
Yes, but with limited numbers.
What qualifications are required?
We welcome applications from undergraduate and graduate students in STEM fields. Strong analytical skills, basic programming knowledge, and enthusiasm for space science are highly valued.
Do I need prior experience?
Not really, it's hand-on training to our interns and assign mentors.
Where is the internship held at?
The internship is held at Entoto, in labs at our office buliding and Combinations depending on the projects.
